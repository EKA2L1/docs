Detailed Description

Simple serialization class.

Using this class, you can serialize your data to buffer, and deserialize it later. The method of serialize is easy to understand, it simply stores your data to buffer as binary.

There are three serialization mode:

  • Read: Deserialize data from buffer.
  • Write: Serialize data to buffer.
  • Measure: Calculate total bytes of buffer needed when data are done serialized.
